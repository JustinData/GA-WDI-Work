About

Hi! My name is Davis. I'm 22 years old and from New York. I currently attend NYU and for the last 4 years of my life have been wondering what career path to choose. For a short while, I pursued law school, then delved into finance, but after much experimenting, I think web development is for me.

I've had fun playing around with HTML, CSS and Ruby on Codeacademy, but those self-teaching methods have limits and I believe it is time to immerse (as in Web Development Immersive haha) myself in code and learn programming full-time so I can become a developer!

On my free time I enjoy playing & watching basketball, watching films, and taking photos. Whether its through TV, streaming, at a bar or the Garden itself, I have to see the Knicks play. I also enjoy going to the local Angellica theater to watch interesting indie movies or watching oldies with friends. If I'm not doing those things then I'm probably walking around with my DSLR (but only when the weather's nice because cameras are too expensive).
